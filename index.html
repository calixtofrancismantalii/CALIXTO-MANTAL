01. About Me

Calixto is working as Web Developer for Everise Marketing Department. He collaborates with the graphic design team and works closely with lead developer to implement web-based projects for the marketing team.


Back then, Calixto worked as a graphic designer for years. He made numerous designs such as logos, posters, and social media posting. He worked as graphic designer of known personality in politics, where his designs are used for campaign and project advertisement. With his years of experience, he put his talent in web design with being able to make it live and published to the world by writing it in codes.


He landed his career as Front-end developer where he made multiple websites for B2B companies. Up to this time, he is pursuing to become a full-stack developer.
